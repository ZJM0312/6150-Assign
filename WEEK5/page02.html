Ready to go

Nanshan Ski Resort is located in Miyun District of Beijing, 62 kilometers away from the North Four Ring Wanghe Bridge. The transportation is very convenient. You can drive directly to the Beijing-Chengcheng Expressway, and there are more than 1500 parking Spaces. The ski resort is located in the north slope of the south Mountain, with excellent snow quality, magnificent scenery and pleasant climate. It is a winter resort in North China integrating skiing, chutes and other dynamic leisure sports. Nanshan Ski Resort is committed to creating a safe and professional skiing venue for the public skiing enthusiasts. After 21 years of continuous development and improvement, it has now built a total of 25 junior high school senior ski runs, teaching runs and two entertainment ski runs, with a reception capacity of more than 10,000 people for matinals and night skiing. The ski resort has independent children's skiing practice track, children's skiing teaching track, big and small wave track, mini park and other entry terraces for children and teenagers to learn skiing.
