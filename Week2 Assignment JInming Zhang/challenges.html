Founded in 1898, we’re renowned for our experiential learning model, high-impact research, deep partnerships, and worldwide reach. From day one we’ve pursued innovative ways of teaching and research that place a premium on experience and engagement with the world. Today, our signature approach erases traditional boundaries, empowering not only students, but faculty, alumni, partners, and innovators to solve problems and pursue impact.We’ve created a one-of-a-kind global university system of campuses, alumni, and partners that forms a dynamic, diverse, and inclusive community. We create new opportunities for richer educational experiences, and deeper collaborations across industries and disciplines to bring the right expertise together to go from promise to reality.Fully integrated and locally informed, our network of campuses provides our community and partners unique opportunities across a vast range of geographic, political, and cultural contexts. The system serves as a platform for scaling ideas, partners, talent, and solutions, making the work of our students, faculty, and collaborators richer and more consequential.
