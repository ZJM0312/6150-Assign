Life for international students at Northeastern University in Boston is a vibrant and enriching experience. NEU is known for its diverse and inclusive community, making it an ideal destination for students from all over the world. Here's a description of what international students can expect during their time at NEU: Academic Excellence: NEU is renowned for its rigorous academic programs and world-class faculty. International students have access to a wide range of majors and minors, and the university's co-op program provides invaluable hands-on work experience. Cultural Diversity: NEU is a melting pot of cultures. International students will find a diverse and welcoming community, allowing them to connect with peers from various backgrounds and gain a global perspective. Support Services: NEU offers comprehensive support services for international students. The International Student and Scholar Institute (ISSI) provides assistance with immigration, cultural adjustment, and social activities, ensuring that international students have the resources they need to succeed. Cultural and Social Activities: NEU hosts a variety of cultural events, international student clubs, and social gatherings throughout the year. These activities provide opportunities to showcase one's own culture, learn about others, and make lasting friendships.
